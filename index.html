Cinema Paradiso Movie Website

As a UI/UX Designer, I spearheaded the creation of an innovative UI/UX design solution for an ed tech platform inspired by popular streaming services like Netflix and Amazon Prime. Drawing on comprehensive user research, I gained valuable insights into student preferences, behaviors, and pain points, guiding data-driven design decisions. Employing industry-leading tools like Figma, I crafted wireframes, mockups, and interactive prototypes to effectively visualize and communicate design ideas. My close collaboration with developers ensured seamless implementation, providing detailed design specifications and guidelines. The result was an intuitive and visually engaging user interface, contributing to an enhanced student learning experience. I also worked closely with the content team to ensure design elements complemented the educational material and learning objectives, creating a cohesive and captivating movie website.
